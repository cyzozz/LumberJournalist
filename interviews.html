Q1: Tell us about yourself
A1: Hello Lumber Fan Group Community, I am Bentley formal Journalist, and Event Committee. I currently do QOTD, and I started Lt2 in 2017-2018. I saw the game and it looked appealing. A2: ERROR911 and CorrupedKey12 introduced me to the game, they were rich and one wanted to help me. Both of these boys are now in their 20s and we all wish we weren't this old because the good times. Although, we still talk and laugh no more Roblox.

Q4: What's your biggest goal in LT2 and why?
A4: My biggest goal is to just help players, I have always been negative in Fan Group and I want to change that. Trying to work my way back up slowly. But if you ever need help DM me or something and I would love to help you.

Q5: If LT2 would ever have a big update, what would you want to see added?
A5: If Lt2 had a BIG update I would want something as in a Map revamp. Defaultio is not ready for a new biome etc. but something small then we can go big. Everyone wants the game to advance but the game is more fun easy and classic style!

Q6: What made you continue playing LT2?
A6: Fan Group, if it wasn't for this server I would have left the game for life. A little motivation to finish my builds, make shops, and just chat in the community made me feel welcome and happy no matter what mood I was in. If I had a bad day I knew I could depend on FG because I trusted them most on my online world!

Q7: Could you give advice for new LT2 players?
A7: Hold on to your builds, before they get massed dupe and make sure you impact those who impacted you. Don't give up, stress, or quit sometimes things can be hard but never giving up is the key to unlock your Lt2 success.
